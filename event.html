It's back. And it's better.

Ask someone what their favorite part of Taiwan is, and chances are the answer will be about food. It’s in this spirit that TAP brings you our Third Night Market – a journey that will transport you to a delightful, one-of-a-kind fair where you’ll be treated to food from some of NYC’s finest vendors – savory, sweet, and delicious.


We'll Have Savory, Sweet, and More to Wash It Down


  • Taicken Chicken

  • Ah-Pou Dumplings

  • Macaron Parlour

  • Thirstea

Also: Sweet Pleasure Confections, Wooly's Ice, Nom Wah, Bite Size Kitchen,
Taiwan Beer, Tiger Beer, And More!


Curated Selection of Food.

With your Night Market Passport in hand, you'll get to try food from all of our delicious hand-picked vendors. Mix, mingle, drink, and journey from savory to sweet as we transform a Williamsburg warehouse into an experience. Venue is located a short walk from the Bedford Avenue L stop and the East River Ferry (North Williamsburg stop).
